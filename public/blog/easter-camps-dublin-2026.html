🐣 Easter Camps Dublin 2026

Find the right camp for your kids — matched by age, filtered by what matters.

Easter 2026 falls on 5th–6th April, with most schools breaking up from 27th March to 10th April. That's two weeks to cover — and if you're a working parent, you need camps that actually fit your schedule.

Here's what you need to know about booking Easter camps in Dublin this year.

When to book

The best camps fill up fast. Based on last year's patterns, here's the timeline:

  • February — early bird registrations open for the big providers (multi-activity, sports camps)
  • Early March — most camps are listed and taking bookings
  • Mid March — popular camps start filling up, especially for the 5-8 age group
  • Late March — last-minute spots only. If your kid's friends are going, it might already be full.
Pro tip: If your child already goes to a club (swimming, GAA, gymnastics), check if that club runs an Easter camp first. Your child already knows the coaches, and they often offer priority booking to existing members.

What to look for

Not all camps are equal. Here's what matters:

  • Age range — a camp for "5-12 year olds" is very different for a 5-year-old vs an 11-year-old. Look for tighter age bands.
  • Hours — does it cover your work day? A camp that runs 10am-1pm doesn't help if you work until 5pm. Look for 9am-3pm minimum.
  • Drop-off flexibility — can you drop off at 8:30am? Is there an extended day option?
  • Indoor/outdoor — it's Easter in Ireland. Have a backup if it's outdoor-only.
  • Cost — Dublin Easter camps typically range from €80-€200 per week. Multi-activity camps are usually better value than specialist ones.

Types of Easter camps in Dublin

  • Multi-activity — sports, arts, games. Good all-rounders. Usually €100-€160/week.
  • Sports-specific — GAA, soccer, rugby, swimming. Great if your child is already into a sport.
  • Creative/arts — drama, art, music. Often shorter days (10am-1pm).
  • STEM/coding — robotics, coding, science. Growing in popularity. €120-€200/week.
  • Outdoor adventure — forest school, adventure camps. Weather-dependent but brilliant when it works.
